Mr. Allan

My name is Mr. Allan, I have a DEC from Dawson College in CRLT (Community Recreational Leadership Training) and a university degree in Human Relations. My role is behavior technician/SpecialEd technician, and i basically teach kids social skills. That's most of my day-to-day routine.

(On difficulties in Special Education) Some of the biggest challenges in today's world of special education are the mental ones. In today's society, there's a ton of anxiety and depression.

(On SpecialEd Technology) Sure, there is tools out there, we use them. For example, technology has done a great deal, such as Visual Accomadations. Lot of students need visual aid.

Finally, I want to say that this is not an easy field, but we do our best to help the students.
