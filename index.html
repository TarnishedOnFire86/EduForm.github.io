Our Goal üéØ

Our current special education system has many defects and it is not able to cater to the needs of all students that require it. Massive issues like financial cuts, lack of professional individuals and infrastructures, and last but not least the uneducated-ness of teachers, parents and even students stand in the way of an inclusive and accessible, quality education for many students. We are here to change that, and we do it by various ways such as the EduForm Website.
