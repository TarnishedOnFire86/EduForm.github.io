Resources in Montreal and Quebec

  • English Montreal School Board - Special Needs Services

    Provides specialized classes, modified curricula, and professional evaluations for students with learning disabilities, social maladjustments, or physical handicaps. Includes programs like SEEDS and WINGS, and publishes *Inspirations* newspaper.

  • Lester B. Pearson School Board - Special Needs Services

    Offers integrated classroom support, individualized education plans, and referrals to specialized programs for students with special needs in Montreal’s West Island.

  • Centre de services scolaire de Montréal - Écoles spécialisées

    Operates specialized schools and programs for students with disabilities or behavioral challenges, providing tailored French-language education.

  • Centre de services scolaire Marguerite-Bourgeoys - Écoles spécialisées

    Provides specialized educational services for students with intellectual disabilities or autism in Montreal’s southwest, emphasizing individualized support.

  • REACH School (Riverside School Board)

    The only English public school for special needs students in Montreal, serving ages 4–21 with small classes and community programs like the McGill Double Challenge.

  • REISA

    Supports English-speaking communities in Eastern/Northern Montreal with access to special needs services and an interactive Resource Guide.

  • Fédération des comités de parents du Québec - Handbook for Parents

    Provides resources and a handbook for parents navigating special education services in Quebec’s education system.
